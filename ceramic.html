System X Max 10 Year Coating
Why a ceramic coating? System X Max is a 10 year coating that locks in long lasting beauty that never fades. Protects from UV rays, salt, dirt, exhaust fumes, contaminents for many years giving your ride the ultimate protection. System X Max provides super slick and glossy paint protection with previously unachieveable levels of durability, hardness, and chemical resistance. Thicker than any coating Max is ready to protect in the most extreme enviorments. Included is a two year coating on glass, trim, and wheels.
